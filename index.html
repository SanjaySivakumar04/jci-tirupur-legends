Welcome to JCI TIRUPUR LEGENDS

Welcome to JCI Zone XVII, a distinguished community within JCI India since 1997. Our heritage is marked by leadership and innovation, featuring prominent figures including one World President, six Past National Presidents, three National Vice Presidents, and more.

We've proudly hosted landmark national events like NALENDA, JASMINE, and ABLE, setting standards in organizational excellence. Our commitment extends through our roles in global and national leadership, including a World JCI Treasurer and three JCI Vice Presidents. Join us as we continue to lead, inspire, and make impactful contributions to our society.

Our Mission

To provide leadership development opportunities that empower young people to create positive change.

Our Vision

To be the foremost global network of young leaders.

What is JCI?

Junior Chamber International (JCI) is a worldwide federation of young leaders and entrepreneurs with nearly five lakh active members and millions of alumni spread across more than 115 countries. We are young active citizens all over the world, living, communicating, taking action and creating impact in our communities.
